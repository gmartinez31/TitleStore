Services

We offer exceptional service, a transparent process, and open communication.

Bonded Title

This type of service is a convenient way to ensure transfer of ownership for a vehicle you possess without a proper title. We take care of the paperwork and handle the entire process so you don't have to deal with the headaches and frustration.

  • The vehicle must be in your possession and cannot be subject to any legal proceedings such as Liens, Foreclosure or Abandonment.
  • Processing usually take 48 to 72 hours to get a title receipt and plates (if required) delivered to your home or place of business.
  • A certified title will be delivered via U. S. Mail within 3 to 4 weeks directly from the state Dept. of Motor Vehicles.

Title Replacement

Need a new title? With this type of service, you obtain a new, certified title copy. Like our Bonded Title service, we handle the entire process and take care of the paperwork for you.

  • You must be the last registered owner or lienholder in order to obtain a certified copy title using this service.
  • Processing usually take 24 to 48 hours to get a title delivered to your home or place of business.

About Us

We're a family-owned title service business with a strong desire to help people.

Founded in Houston, TX in 1995, our initial purpose was to provide local vehicle dealers with a way to reach us for service and provide their employees with detailed information and instructions on the processing of documents through the Harris County Tax Assessors Annex.

As time passed and we became more aware of our sites exposure to the general public, we began our long process of fine-tuning our site to better serve our customers. What you see today is the outcome of those endeavors. It has been a long journey to reach this point, but we are very proud of our site and the services we have produced thus far.

Our goal is to make our service hassle and worry free for you. In our consultation we will take the time to make sure you understand how our services works, what paperwork we will provide, detailed service instructions needed for successfully completing your transaction through your local state and county offices, and a comprehensive estimate on what state or county fees are involved along with a flat rate fee for our services.
